I'm a mechanical engineer with a passion for creating innovative solutions across aerospace and motorsports. I'll welcome any challenge that is thrown at me and am excited to apply myself to any task required.

I've also worked on shape-memory-alloy tensegrity structures for my senior capstone, and contributed to publications and studies at NASA.
